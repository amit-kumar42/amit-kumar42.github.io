Why Choose Beginner Track?

The Beginner Track is designed for freshers and aspiring software developers who are ready to take their first step into the professional world of software development. This track focuses on building a strong foundation in project development, industry tools, and professional skills to make participants product and team ready.

What You'll Gain

Hands-On Projects

Build a real-world application, such as a task manager or expense tracker, under mentor guidance.

Professional Tools

Get familiar with industry-standard tools for collaborative development and version control.

Global Mentorship

Learn from experienced mentors with international expertise and guidance.

Program Details

Key Highlights

  • Duration: 4-6 weeks of immersive learning.
  • Commitment: 15-20 hours per week.
  • Project Focus: Build a beginner-friendly application.
  • Certification: Earn a Certificate of Completion.

Structure

  • Interactive live sessions with mentors.
  • Step-by-step project development.
  • Hands-on experience with SDLC fundamentals.
  • Collaborative learning and peer interaction.
