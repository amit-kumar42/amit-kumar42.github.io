Why Choose the Intermediate Track?

The Intermediate Track is for developers with foundational experience looking to enhance project delivery, lead teams, and transition into project management. Learn to deliver user-focused projects with seamless workflows and effective leadership.

Key Learning Outcomes

Advanced Projects

Develop software projects that mirror real-world environments using industry tools.

User Centric Design

Design products that prioritize user and customer needs for seamless and impactful solutions.

Project Management

Master effort estimation, project planning, and end-to-end execution.

Leadership

Build team collaboration and leadership skills for collaborative success.

Program Details

Duration & Commitment

  • Duration: 4-6 weeks of immersive learning.
  • Weekly Commitment: 15-20 hours.
  • Format: Live sessions, hands-on project work, and mentor feedback.

Program Features

  • Hands-on intermediate-level project development.
  • Simulations for user and client interaction.
  • Leadership training for team-building success.
  • Detailed project management training with effort estimation and planning.
